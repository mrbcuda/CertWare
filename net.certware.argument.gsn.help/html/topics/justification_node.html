Justification Nodes

Background

Strategies often need some justification for their use. It may be that the strategy is laid down in some standard followed by the developers, e.g. ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code Section III recommends that it be done; it may be common practice; or it may be a more elaborate argument as to the validity of the use of the strategy. Alternatively a justification may call upon evidence from analysis of the model or be a structured proof. A Justification is used to give a rationale for the use or satisfaction of a particular Argument or Strategy. More generally it can be used to justify the change that is the subject of the Safety Case.

Application

To add a justification node to the case argument model, right-click on a Strategy node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Justification. Select the new justification node and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
