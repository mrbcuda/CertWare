Goal Nodes

Background

A goal is a requirement, target or constraint to be met by the system or parts of it. Goals are intended to be sufficiently general so that they can also capture process issues, e.g. develop to the requirements of Interim Defence Standard 00-55. A goal hierarchy refers to the collection of goals produced by hierarchical decomposition into sub-goals. A Goal is usually "owned" by some stakeholder.

Application

To add a goal node to the case argument model, right-click on a Goal or Strategy node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Goal. Select the new goal and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
