Justification Nodes

Background

The various elements of the GSN can all be given justifications for their use. Justifications are most frequently associated with strategies.

Application

To add a justification node to the case argument model, right-click on an Argument, Strategy, or Solution node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Justification. Select the new justification node and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
