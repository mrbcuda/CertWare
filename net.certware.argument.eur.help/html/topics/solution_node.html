Solution Nodes

Background

Solutions provide the evidence for satisfaction of goals. They may be individual pieces of analysis, evidence, results of audit reports, references to design material, etc.

Application

To add a solution node to the case argument model, right-click on a Goal node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Solution. Select the new solution node and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
