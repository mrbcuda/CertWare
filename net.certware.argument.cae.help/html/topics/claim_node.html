Claim Nodes

Background

The safety case argument in CAE notation starts with claims, and then decomposes these claims into sub-claims, arguments, and evidence.

Application

To add a claim node to the case argument model, right-click on a Claim or Argument node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Claim. Select the new strategy node and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
