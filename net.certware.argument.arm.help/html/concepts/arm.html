Argumentation Metamodel (ARM) Notation

Background

The ARM notation is an OMG-provided reconciliation of the goal-structuring notation (GSN) and the claim-argument-evidence (CAE) notation. With the ARM metamodel one is able to produce argument models following the GSN or CAE conventions, or produce a notation specific to a particular application.
